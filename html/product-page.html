50 Series is the top of the line in the Lowden range, employing master grade tone-woods and the finest of appointments with upgrades in the rosette, back-strip, fretboard extension, and book-matched face plate and back plate on the headstock.

The Lowden F-32 guitar can do it all, from delicate fingerstyle to aggressive strumming! An excellent all-around guitar.
